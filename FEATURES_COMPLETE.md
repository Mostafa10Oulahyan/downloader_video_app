## 📸 To Share a Screenshot

Since you asked how to put a screenshot in the terminal:

**Option 1: Take Screenshot**
1. Press `Win + Shift + S` on Windows
2. Select the area to capture
3. Screenshot saved to clipboard

**Option 2: Share via File**
1. Take screenshot with `Print Screen`
2. Save as PNG/JPG
3. Upload to image hosting (Imgur, etc.)
4. Share the link!

**For Claude Code:**
- Screenshots can be dragged into the chat
- Or paste from clipboard directly
- File paths work if screenshot is saved locally
